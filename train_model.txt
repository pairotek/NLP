========= add message negative =======

step 1. go to ./SentimentAnalysisProduction/negative_messages and add message negative for train and test model.

      $ vi ./SentimentAnalysisProduction/negative_messages/train_neg.txt

      $ vi ./SentimentAnalysisProduction/negative_messages/test_neg.txt
     
   
========= add message positive =======
      
step 2. go to ./SentimentAnalysisProduction/positive_messages and add message positive for train and test model.

      $ vi ./SentimentAnalysisProduction/positive_messages/train_pos.txt
     
      $ vi ./SentimentAnalysisProduction/positive_messages/test_pos.txt
      

========= train model =======
      
step 5. go to ./SentimentAnalysisProduction for train model
      
      $ python3 ./SentimentAnalysisProduction/dl_model.py
      *** After run model about 5-6 Hr. It will ganerate model file thai_sentiment_final.h5 and tokenizer.pickle and take it to predict on api.
